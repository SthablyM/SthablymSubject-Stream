🇿🇦 Our Story

Built by South Africans,
For South African Learners

Sthablym was born from a simple problem: too many Grade 9 learners choose the wrong subjects and only discover the consequences in Grade 12. We set out to fix that.

The Problem We Solve

The Subject Choice Crisis in SA Schools

Every year, thousands of Grade 9 learners in South Africa choose their subject streams based on incomplete information — peer pressure, what's "cool," or what their parents suggest — without understanding the APS consequences for university admission.

By Grade 12, it's often too late. Students find themselves locked out of the careers they want because of subject choices made three years earlier.

Stablym gives learners the information they need — upfront, clearly, and in a format they actually engage with — to make informed decisions that open doors rather than close them.
